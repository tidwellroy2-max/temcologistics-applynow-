Role Responsibilities

Accounts Payable & Receivable Specialist
  • Process and manage accounts payable transactions (invoices, vendor payments, expense reports)
  • Monitor accounts receivable and follow up on outstanding invoices and reconcile bank statements and financial discrepancies
  • Maintain accurate financial records and documentation also prepare and send invoices to clients/customers
 Customer Care Representative
  • Respond to customer inquiries via phone, email, or chat
  • Resolve service issues professionally and efficiently
  • Maintain accurate records of customer interactions
Social Media Marketing Assistant
  • Maintain a consistent posting calendar and brand voice
  • Engage with followers to build relationships and increase brand loyalty
  • Assist with ad campaigns, influencer outreach, and marketing strategies
 Remote Administrative Coordinator
  • Coordinate schedules, meetings, and internal communications
  • Maintain operational and financial documentation
  • Support reporting and cross-department collaboration
